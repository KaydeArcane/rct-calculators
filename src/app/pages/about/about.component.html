RCT Calculators

A collection of useful calculators for use with OpenRCT2, Roller Coaster Tycoon 1, Roller Coaster Tycoon 2, and Roller Coaster Tycoon Classic.

Data sourced from OpenRCT2 repo.
Ride price calculations and soft guest cap calculations based on discoveries by Deurklink.
Massive shout out to Marcel Vos for covering these mechanics on YouTube and spurring my interest in making this!
